MVP CLL Phase 1 : Guidelines for Formatting PDF Files for CHL AI Assistant

Created by Dương Thị Cúc, last modified on Nov 24, 2023

This section provides instructions on formatting PDF files to ensure optimal operation and accurate responses from the CHL AI Assistant chatbot. The transformation of PDF File data into text, exclusive of other data types, is crucial. Follow the instructions below to properly reformat data in tables, figures, and paragraphs for your PDF files:

1. Figure's Context
To prevent information clutter and ensure clarity in content, please adhere to the following:

  • Remove Disjointed Figures.
  • Eliminate figures that lack clarity or are disjointed from the main content.

2. Paragraphs Division
Maintain a well-structured layout for paragraphs to enhance readability and avoid confusion. Follow these guidelines:

  • Ensure that the heading of the second paragraph is not located within the first paragraph.
  • Correct Line Spacing: Verify that line spacing within paragraphs and between different paragraphs is consistent to prevent confusion.
  • Reformat Data for Optimal Spacing: Adjust the spacing between lines of different paragraphs to be greater than the spacing between lines within the same paragraph.

3. Instructions for Reformatting Table data

a. Table Format:

To optimize table data, follow these guidelines:

  • Add Tags:

    • Users should add the <table> and </table> tags to pages with table data.
    • Ensure the <table> tag is above and separated from the table by a blank line.
    • The </table> tag should be immediately under the table.

  • Context Placement:

    • All contexts of one table must be on the same page.

b. Rule for Title Location:

When setting the location for the title of the table:

  • The title must belong to the text immediately above the table.
  • The title must be separated from the previous text by a blank line.

By adhering to these instructions, you contribute to the efficient processing of your PDF files by the chatbot, resulting in accurate and meaningful responses. If you have any questions or require further assistance, consult our support documentation or reach out to our customer support team. Thank you for enhancing your chatbot experience!
