MVP CLL Phase 1 : Terms uploading the URL Link for Specialized Bot

Created by Dương Thị Cúc on Nov 24, 2023

Welcome to the URL Link uploading feature for your Specialized Bot! This guide will provide you with the terms, conditions, and guidelines for successfully uploading URL links.

  • Language Requirement:
    • The content of each page must be in English, or the minimum percentage of English content is 60% (only English parts will be processed).
  • Permitted Action:
    • You can upload only one URL Link, provided that the site allows data crawling.
    • You can crawl a single page If you do not click the checkbox "All relevant pages".
    • You can choose to crawl the entire site by clicking the checkbox "All relevant pages". The maximum crawling limit for a bot is 100 pages related.
    • You have a maximum of 2 times to update the dataset for each chatbot.
    • When updating data, the system replaces all old data with new data, ensuring that existing data is preserved before deploying the new data to users.
  • Restricted Actions:
    • Importing pages that do not allow crawling or require handling/authentication is not allowed.

By adhering to these terms and guidelines, you ensure the successful and effective utilization of URL Link data for your Personal Chatbot. If you have any questions or need further assistance, consult our support documentation or reach out to our customer support team. Thank you for enhancing your chatbot experience!
