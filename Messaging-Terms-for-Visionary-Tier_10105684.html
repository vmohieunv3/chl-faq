MVP CLL Phase 1 : Messaging Terms for Visionary Tier

Created by Dương Thị Cúc, last modified on Nov 29, 2023

Welcome to the messaging features of our platform! This guide provides you with the terms and limitations associated with sending messages to your AI chatbot in the Visionary tier.

  • Access:

    • Access to Charlie Assistant.
    • Access to 5 free AI Personal Chatbots.
    • Access to 3 Specialized AI Chatbots.

  • Limitations:

    • Monthly messages limited to 500 for all chatbots (Counting only messages sent by the user to the bot).
    • Each message has a maximum limit of 1500 characters.

By following these terms and guidelines, you can make the most out of your chatbot interactions based on your subscription tier. If you have any questions or need further assistance, consult our support documentation or reach out to our customer support team. Thank you for engaging with our chatbots!
