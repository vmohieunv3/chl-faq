MVP CLL Phase 1 : AI Personal Chatbot

Created by Dương Thị Cúc, last modified on Nov 29, 2023

Welcome to the Chatbot Creation and Update feature! This guide will help you understand the terms, actors, and limitations associated with creating and updating your AI Personal chatbot.

  • You can create/ update many Personal chatbots within the limits of your user tier.
  • If your tier is Explorer Tier, you can create 1 AI Personal chatbot.
  • If your tier is Creator Tier, you can create 3 AI Personal chatbots.
  • If your tier is Visionary Tier, you can create 5 AI Personal chatbots.
  • Total the times to create and update the dataset a maximum of 3 times for each chatbot (maximum 2 times update dataset of chatbot.

By following these terms and guidelines, you can efficiently create and update your chatbots within the specified limits of your user tier. If you have any questions or need further assistance, consult our support documentation or reach out to our customer support team. Thank you for enhancing your chatbot experience!
